KHMER DANCE

At the heart of classical form is the Apsara, the joyful, almost wanton dancer whose images are everywhere. Princess Buppha Devi, who currently serves as the Minister of Culture, is a master of Apsara dancing, which dates to the 1st century.

KHMER CEREMONY

Khmer New Year is known to celebrate in the mid-April every year from 13-15. It is one of the national ceremonies that not rarely miss doing and it is also a meeting the time between family members who move to work or live further from home.

KHMER MUSIC

Khmer music consists of polyphonic stratification and is based predominantly on the pentatonic (five-tone) scale. It is built linearly, devoid of harmony in the Western sense. Musicians in a music ensemble have a collective melody in mind that no single musician actually plays.
